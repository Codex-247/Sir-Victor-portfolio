A Vibrant Apostolic Voice

Rev. Victor Ifeanyi Anaele stands as one of the vibrant apostolic voices emerging in Nigeria. He is the Resident Pastor of Remnant Christian Network (RCN), Uyo, a city to which the Lord divinely directed him after fruitful years of ministry service.

Before his assignment in Uyo, he faithfully served as RCN's point man in Abia State, where he planted and nurtured apostolic centers. Rev. Victor Anaele has always carried a burden for regional transformation through the Gospel.

His ministry is characterized by clarity, doctrinal soundness, and a deep passion for raising altars of righteousness across nations. Through his teachings and leadership, countless lives have been transformed and revived with a fresh hunger for God's presence.

Ministry Journey

2013-2014

Early Leadership Formation

During his undergraduate years, Rev. Victor rose to serve as Faculty President of NIFES and later Zonal President of NIFES Calabar. These formative leadership roles deepened his hunger for revival and laid a strong foundation for his later apostolic calling.

2017

Joining Remnant Christian Network

Rev. Victor joined the Remnant Christian Network under the apostolic leadership of Apostle Arome Osayi. This marked the beginning of his formal apostolic ministry and his burden for raising altars of righteousness.

2017-Present

Apostolic Center Establishment

His burden for restoring priesthood led him to pioneer and establish six RCN apostolic centers in strategic cities, including Aba, Owerri, Port Harcourt, Bayelsa, Umuahia, and now Uyo. Through these platforms, he has impacted countless lives across the world.

Present

Resident Pastor - RCN Uyo

Currently serving as the Resident Pastor of Remnant Christian Network (RCN), Uyo, where he continues to raise altars of righteousness and restore priesthood, reviving a fresh hunger for God's presence and truth.

Leadership & Impact

Rev. Victor Anaele is the convener of major intercessory gatherings such as The Eastern Watchmen Convergence and The Prophetic Convergence, bringing together believers for regional transformation through prayer and spiritual warfare.

Educational & Outreach Ministry

In addition to his pastoral duties, Rev. Victor spearheads the Remnant Foundation School (RFS), an intensive training hub designed to equip believers with biblical literacy and spiritual capacity. This educational initiative has become a cornerstone of his ministry approach.

He has also extended his ministry into rural missions and youth outreach, organizing school and community interventions across the South–South and South–East regions of Nigeria, bringing the Gospel to underserved communities.

Personal Life & Family

Beyond his ministry calling, Rev. Victor Anaele is a devoted family man who exemplifies the biblical principles of love, commitment, and godly leadership in his personal life.

Family

Rev. Victor Anaele is happily married to Mrs. Comfort Anaele, and together they are blessed with two sons, Jason (Zinachimdindu) and Jephthah. Their family serves as a testament to the power of godly relationships and the importance of raising the next generation in the fear of the Lord.

The Anaele family continues to be a source of inspiration to many, demonstrating how ministry and family life can beautifully complement each other in service to God's kingdom.
